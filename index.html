About the Event

Quantum Information and Quantum Technology - 2023 (QIQT -2023) is the fourth in the series of workshops conducted in the summer months at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Kolkata (IISER-K). It intends to bring together the leading experts in the field face to face with the students and young researchers in this exciting field of research. The experts include Physicists, Chemists, Mathematicians, Computer Scientists, and Engineers.

Each year a focus area is identified, keeping the international trend in mind, QIQT -2023 intends to focus on Quantum Optics, Quantum Chemistry, Quantum Foundation, and Quantum Materials. The workshop will also include "Researchers' Meet" highlighting the works of young researchers of India in the broad domain of Quantum Optics, Quantum Chemistry, Quantum Foundation, and Quantum Materials.
